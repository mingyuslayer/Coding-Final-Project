Tips for Better Sleep Hygiene

  • Stick to a regular sleep schedule: Go to bed and wake up at the same time every day.
  • Avoid screens before bed: Turn off your phone, tablet, or computer at least 30 minutes before sleep.
  • Limit caffeine and alcohol: Avoid consuming these in the hours leading up to bedtime.
  • Create a restful environment: Keep your bedroom dark, quiet, and at a comfortable temperature.
  • Relax before bed: Consider a bedtime routine like reading, meditation, or deep breathing exercises.
